Fun88’s Responsible Gambling Policy

Fun88 is committed to endorsing responsible wagering as well as promoting awareness of the problem of gambling and improving prevention, intervention and treatment. fun88 ’s Responsible Gambling Policy sets out its commitment to minimise the negative effects of problem gambling and to promote responsible gambling practices. We believe it is our responsibility to you, our customers, to ensure that you enjoy your wagering experience on our site while remaining fully aware of the social and financial harms associated with gambling. Being in this business for years, we are aware of the causes and effects of all sorts of gambling, online and offline. In order to assist our players to gamble responsibly, we ensure that all our staff receive training in all aspects of responsible gambling. Please contact our Helpdesk if you require further information or assistance.

Maintaining Control

Whilst the majority of our customers gamble within their means for some it can be more difficult. In order to maintain control of your gambling habits we would like you to bear in mind the following points:

1. Gambling should be done in moderation and undertaken as a form of leisure not as a genuine way of making money

2. Avoid chasing losses

3. Only gamble when you can cover the losses

4. Keep track of the time and monitor the amount of money you spend

If you need to talk to someone about any concerns you may have with your gambling, please contact one of the organisations which we have detailed below.

Underage Gambling

It is illegal for anyone under the age of 21 to open an account or to gamble on fun88. Fun88 takes its responsibilities in this regard very seriously. We carry out age verification checks on all customers who use payment mechanisms which are available to those under 21’s and additionally perform random age verification checks on customers using other forms of funding mechanisms. PLEASE NOTE THAT ANYONE UNDER THE AGE OF 21 FOUND TO BE USING THIS SITE WILL HAVE ANY WINNINGS FORFEITED AND MAY ALSO BE REPORTED TO THE POLICE.

Filtering Systems

fun88 advises and encourages its customers to prevent minors from accessing gambling websites. We understand the bad effects and consequences of gambling addiction on families, youth, and other sections of society. ICRA (the Internet Content Rating Association) offers a complete filtering solution for parents and fun88 is registered with them as a ‘gambling content’ site. If you share your computer with friends or family who are under the legal age to register or bet with our site, please click on the link to register: www.icra.org. Alternative filtering solutions are available from NetNanny or cyberpatrol.
